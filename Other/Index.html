Our Menu

Burger

Summer is here, which means lots of outdoor parties, grilling and of course burgers! This Indian style Vegetarian Burger is loaded with veggies & flavored with spices like garam masala and cumin! The mixed vegetable patties are so flavorful and the spiced mayonnaise sauce makes it even better! These burgers are perfect for grilling at your next summer BBQ.

Chicken

Butter Chicken is one of the most popular curries at any Indian restaurant around the world. Aromatic golden chicken pieces in an incredible creamy curry sauce, this Butter Chicken recipe is one of the best you will try! You will love how easy it is to make in the comfort of your own home, especially with garlic butter rice and fresh homemade Naan bread.

Dhokla

Dhokla is made with ground, fermented batter of rice and lentils like chana dal or urad dal. You can check my recipe for an authentic Dhokla made with rice and chana dal. While khaman is made instantly with gram flour. Khaman batter is not fermented unlike a dhokla batter.
